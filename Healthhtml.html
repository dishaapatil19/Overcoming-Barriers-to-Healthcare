Fear of Diagnosis

Psychological Avoidance Profile
Why This Happens

Fear is a core survival response. In healthcare, this manifests as *information avoidance*. People skip tests because they sub-consciously prefer the uncertainty over a confirmed diagnosis, falsely believing they are delaying the illness itself.

Overcoming the Barrier

Focus on control: Knowing early puts you in the driver's seat. Early-stage issues are far more treatable, less costly, and less disruptive than late-stage emergencies.
